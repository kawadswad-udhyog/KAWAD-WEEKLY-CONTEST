Our Foundation

Kawad Swad Udhyog is a dedicated family-run papad manufacturing unit based in **Katargaon**, built on daily hard work, consistency, and a clear focus on quality.

Every papad is a result of meticulous **guthai** and **mathai**. We believe that the authentic taste of Nimar comes from following traditional methods without any shortcuts.

100% Quality Focus

🏭

Direct Production

We maintain complete control over our manufacturing unit. Direct supervision at every step ensures that each batch delivers the same crispness and spice level that our customers love.

🚫🍚

Zero Rice Mission

Authentic Nimari papad should be pure. We **do not use rice** in our products. This commitment to purity ensures a superior flavor profile and traditional handmade texture.
