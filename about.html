Our Story

"स्वाद भरा सफ़र" - A Journey of Consistency

Our Foundation

Kawad Swad Udhyog is a family-run papad unit based in **Katargaon**, built on daily hard work, consistency, and a clear focus on quality.

Our work starts from the basics — proper guthai, correct mathai, and balanced ingredients. We believe that the real taste of papad comes from the process, not shortcuts. Every batch is prepared with attention so that the same taste is delivered every day.

100% Quality Focus

🤝

The Core Team

We operate with a small, dedicated team of 3–4 members, including family. This hands-on approach helps us maintain complete control over quality and consistency at every single step.

🚫🍚

The Zero Rice Mission

Authentic taste is our priority. We do not use rice in our papad. This allows the core ingredients to bring out a stronger flavor and a much better texture for our customers.

Respect & Responsibility

"Kawad Swad Udhyog is registered in our mother’s name, reflecting the family-driven foundation of this business. It represents not just ownership, but responsibility and trust."
